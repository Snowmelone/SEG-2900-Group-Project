Overall Look and Feel of Lens
===============================

The overall look and feel should be futuristic/modern while also minimalist. There should be a sense of 3D with the models but the interface portion should be very simple and straightforward.
The website should be simple and easy to follow for users.

Colours
========
For Lens, we aim to use colours that present a very calm and soothing atmosphere.
Colour swatches are not specified
Theme colours are:
  - Black
  - White
  - Blue
  - Purple
  - Grey

Iconography
===========
We will focus on using icons that have a very minimalist design but also relate to our products. 
We aim to be very straightforward with our icon designs

Typography
==========
We will be using a font called "Jura"

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be flat designed using the chosen colors outlined above. The buttons shapes will be either straight rectangles or rectangles with rounded corners
